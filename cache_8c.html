void ye_pre_cache_scene ( json_t *  scene)

Pre-caches a scene.

Iterates through a .yoyo scene file and caches all of its textures. Any textures that can be reused from the old scene will be carried over without reloading.

Parameters
sceneThe scene to pre-cache.
